Positioning Narrative

Traditional Marketing

Traditional marketing developed as businesses needed a way to reach large audiences through the few available channels like newspapers, radio, and TV. It helped companies distinguish their products in an increasingly competitive market and build trust with consumers by using established, reliable media platforms.

Digital Marketing

Digital marketing emerged with the rise of the internet and advanced technology, offering businesses more direct and personalized ways to reach their target audiences. It allows for real-time interaction, data-driven strategies, and global reach at lower costs, making it ideal for modern, fast-paced consumer behavior.
